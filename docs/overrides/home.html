TASSEL 5 (Trait Analysis by aSSociation, Evolution and Linkage)

About TASSEL 5

TASSEL is a software package to evaluate trait associations, evolutionary patterns, and linkage disequilibrium. Strengths of this software:

  1. It provides powerful statistical approaches for association mapping such as General Linear Model (GLM) and Mixed Linear Model (MLM). MLM is a method described in a Nature Genetics paper - Unified Mixed-Model Method for Association Mapping - that reduces Type I error in complex pedigrees and population structures.
  2. TASSEL supports a wide range of indels (insertions & deletions), which are commonly ignored by other software. This is crucial in species like maize where indels are a dominant form of polymorphism.

Citation
